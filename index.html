Create a Bucket

"Buckets" are containers for data. Retention policies are:

  • Transient: 24 hours.
  • Temporary: 30 days.
  • Persistent: until deleted.

Must be between 3 to 128 characters and contain only lowercase letters, numbers and the symbols . _ -
